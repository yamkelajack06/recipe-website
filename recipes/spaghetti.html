How to make Spaghetti

To make spaghetti, start by boiling a large pot of salted water. Once it’s boiling, add the spaghetti noodles and cook according to the package instructions, usually 8-10 minutes. While the pasta cooks, prepare the sauce. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat, then sauté garlic until fragrant. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and any herbs you like (basil or oregano work well). Let the sauce simmer for about 10-15 minutes.

Once the pasta is done, drain it and add it to the pan with the sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly, then serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top. Enjoy your delicious homemade spaghetti!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti noodles
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Step-by-Step Method for Making Spaghetti

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package instructions (8-10 minutes).
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic to the pan and sauté until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the tomato sauce and stir in salt and pepper. Let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Drain the cooked spaghetti and add it to the pan with the sauce. Toss to coat the noodles evenly.
  6. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese on top, if desired.
  7. Enjoy your homemade spaghetti!
