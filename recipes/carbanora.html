How to make Spaghetti Carbonara

To make spaghetti carbonara, cook spaghetti noodles in a large pot of salted water according to package instructions. While the pasta cooks, heat a pan over medium heat and cook diced pancetta or bacon until crispy. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some of the pasta water and then drain the noodles.

Add the hot pasta to the pan with the crispy pancetta, then quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture, adding pasta water a little at a time to achieve a creamy texture. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top. Enjoy your creamy and delicious spaghetti carbonara!

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti noodles
  • 150g pancetta or bacon, diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Salt for boiling pasta
  • Pasta water (reserved from boiling noodles)

Step-by-Step Method for Making Spaghetti Carbonara

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions (8-10 minutes).
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat a pan over medium heat and cook the diced pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together eggs, grated Parmesan cheese, and black pepper until well combined.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, reserve some of the pasta water and drain the noodles.
  5. Add the hot pasta to the pan with crispy pancetta and mix well.
  6. Quickly stir in the egg and cheese mixture, adding reserved pasta water until the sauce reaches a creamy consistency.
  7. Serve with extra Parmesan and black pepper on top. Enjoy your creamy spaghetti carbonara!
