How to make Homemade Lasagna

Making homemade lasagna starts with preparing a rich meat sauce. Brown ground beef or Italian sausage in a pan with diced onions and garlic, then drain any excess fat. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste, seasoning with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil. Let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes to develop deep flavors. Meanwhile, mix ricotta cheese with an egg, grated Parmesan, and chopped parsley to create a creamy filling. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions, then drain and lay them flat to prevent sticking.

To assemble, spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, followed by a layer of noodles, then the ricotta mixture, and a sprinkle of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a final topping of sauce and mozzarella. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for about 30 minutes, then uncover and bake for another 10-15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing to help the layers set, then serve and enjoy!

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce

  • 450g ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic , minced
  • 1 can of crushed tomato sauce
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil

For the Ricotta Mixture

  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 table spoons of chopped fresh parsley (or 1 teaspoon dried parsley)

Other Ingridients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil (for cooking the meat)

Step-by-Step Method for the Making Lasagna

  1. Prepare the meat Sauce

    • Heat a pan over medium heat and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
    • Add the diced onion and minced garlic, sautéing until fragrant.
    • Add the ground beef or Italian sausage and cook until browned.
    • Drain excess fat, then stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste
    • Season with salt, pepper, oregano, and basil.
    • Let the sauce simmer for at least 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  2. Prepare the Ricotta Mixture

    • In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, grated Parmesan, and chopped parsley until well combined.

  3. Cook the Lasagna noodles

    • Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
    • Drain and lay noodles flat on a clean surface to prevent sticking

  4. Assemble the Lasagna

    • Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom.
    • Add a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by a layer of the ricotta mixture.
    • Sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
    • Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top.

  5. Bake the Lasagna

    • Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

  6. Let it Rest and Serve

    • Remove the lasagna from the oven and let it rest for 10 minutes.
    • Slice and serve warm. Enjoy!
